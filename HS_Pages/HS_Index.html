A brief history of the Historical Society:

The first gathering of like minded folks for a historical society took place in 1969, with dilegent work the society recieved its permanent Charter from the NYS Dept. of Education on Feb. 26, 1976 and our designation as an IRS 501(c)(3) charity in March,1977

Our museum consists of 13 park like acres with 10 buildings. Representing a 19th century community. The Heritage Square Museum is owned and operated by The Town of Ontario Historical and Landmark Preservation Society.  The Society is chartered by the New York State Department of Education and is an IRS determined 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
